Tribute to A.P.J Abdul Kalam

About A.P.J Abdul Kalam

A.P.J Abdul Kalam, the"Misssile Man of India" was born on October 15,1931. in Rameshwaram Temple Nadu. He served as a President of India and was a renowned scientist. known for his work in india's space and missile programs.

Achievements

  • Devoloped India's first satellite launch vehicle (SLV-III)
  • Led India's successful nuclear tests in 1998
  • Wrote books like "Wings of Fire" and "India 2020"
  • Recieved the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award
